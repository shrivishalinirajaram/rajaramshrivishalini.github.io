Welcome to my space

From a small town in India to pursuing cutting-edge research in the United States, my journey has been one of persistence, vision, and a relentless drive to follow my passion for science. Growing up with a dream to contribute to the global scientific community, I navigated challenges with determination and a belief in the transformative power of curiosity and hard work. Today, as a doctoral researcher, I explore oral microbiomics, computational biology, and toxicology to address critical questions about human health, integrating complex data to uncover actionable insights.

I believe in the integrity of science—not just as a pursuit of discovery but as a responsibility to society. My work is guided by principles that I deeply care about: the importance of accurate data, the elimination of misinformation, and the accessibility of scientific knowledge for all. Whether through interdisciplinary collaborations, presenting my research to diverse audiences, or mentoring the next generation of thinkers, I am committed to making science not only impactful but also inclusive and empowering.

This space is a reflection of that belief: a hub to share my journey, my research, and my dedication to driving progress through collaboration, communication, and innovation. Whether you are a fellow scientist, a professional, or simply curious about the intersection of biology, technology, and health, thank you for being part of this mission.
