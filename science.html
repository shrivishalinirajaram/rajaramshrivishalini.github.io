Science Communication

Science for all

I believe Science is for all. Every person deserves the right to know the nuances of a disease, how a chemical interacts with our body, why we feel sick, and more. Some are passionate, gifted, and curious to perform experiments, run analyses, and understand mechanisms more closely. These people need to convey complex scientific concepts to the general public in a way they can understand.

These are some of my contributions toward making science accessible and engaging.
